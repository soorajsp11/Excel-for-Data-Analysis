Objective:

	Vrinda store wants to create a annual sales report for 2022. So that Vrinda can understand their customers and grow more sales in 2023.

refernce: Full project in Excel with Intercative Dashboards | Rishabh Mishra

Dataset Link: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1N_Aa45UnkPmnwGqNr6oTzvUhb5TKlitM/edit?pli=1#gid=1081836936

Steps:
 1- Loading dataset into Excel.
 2- Data Cleaning.
 3- Data Analysis.
 4- Dashboard/Report.



Questions:
1- Compare the sales and orders using single chart.
2- Which month got highest sales and orders.
3- Who purchased more- men or women in 2022?
4- What are differnet order status in 2022?
5- List top 10 states contributing to the sales?
6- Relation between age and gender based on number of order?
7- Which channel is contributing to maximum sales?
8- Highest salling category?, etc

Sample Insights:
1- Womens are more likely to buy compared to men (~64%).
2- Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 states.
3- Adult age group (19 to 60 yrs) havving maximum purchase (~50%).
4- Max contributing channels - Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra (~ 80%).

Final conclusion to improve Vrinda Store Sales:
- Target women customers from adult age group from states like Maharashtra, Karnataka and UP by offering them Coupons/Discounts/Offers.
